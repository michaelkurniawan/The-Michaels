    ABOUT

    The Zen Dragon Academy stands as a beacon of hope on the Isle of Berk, offering a sanctuary where Vikings learn the art of harmonious coexistence with dragons. Founded by Sven and his companions, the academy serves as a place of learning and understanding, where humans and dragons forge bonds of friendship and trust. Here, students embark on a journey of discovery, honing their skills in dragon riding and communication, while fostering a deep respect for these majestic creatures. As the first of its kind, the academy represents a bold step towards a future where peace and harmony prevail between two once-warring species.
